Welcome to Loan Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and financial information. By using Loan Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We collect the following types of personal information:

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile picture (optional)
- Account preferences and settings

**Financial Information:**
- Loan details (lender names, amounts, interest rates, terms)
- Payment information and schedules
- Default and penalty information
- Outstanding balances and payment history
- EMI calculations and financial projections

**Device Information:**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Device identifiers (for security purposes)
- IP address (temporarily, for security)

**Usage Information:**
- App usage patterns and features accessed
- Time spent in the app
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance analytics

### Biometric Information
- Face ID or Touch ID data (stored locally on your device only)
- We do not have access to your biometric data
- Biometric authentication is processed entirely on your device

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

**Encryption:**
- All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
- Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
- Local device storage is encrypted

**Access Controls:**
- Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only
- Multi-factor authentication for internal systems
- Regular security audits and assessments

**Biometric Security:**
- Face ID/Touch ID data never leaves your device
- We cannot access your biometric information
- Authentication is processed locally

### Data Storage
- Data is stored on secure servers provided by Firebase/Google Cloud
- Servers are located in secure data centers with physical security
- Regular backups are maintained with encryption
- Data retention policies are enforced

### Data Retention
We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:
- Account information: Until account deletion
- Financial data: Until account deletion or as required by law
- Usage analytics: Aggregated data may be retained longer
- You can request data deletion at any time
